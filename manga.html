Yoshihiro Togashi, the mangaka began its ongoing serialization in Weekly Shōnen Jump magazine on March 16, 1998. Although the manga has frequently gone on hiatuses, 390 chapters have been published till date.

Tokyo Ghoul author Sui Ishida created a 69 page storyboard of a manga chapter depicting the past of Hunter X Hunter's Hisoka. The storyboard was released digitally via Shonen Jump+ on June 2, 2016.

Kurapika's Memories, a two-part manga Yoshihiro Togashi wrote to act as a prequel to the first animated film, was published in the December 3 ,2012 and December 10, 2012 issues of Weekly Shōnen Jump.
