The Dark Continent Arc

The Dark Continent Arc is the latest arc of the series which is yet to be animated. Although the story of GON (protagonist) is over, yet the story of his other friend, Kurapika is still incomplete as he's still on his quest to find the lost eyes of his fallen clan. The awaited fight between Hisoka and Chrollo also takes place and it is yet to see if we get the old friends (GON and KILLUA) back. Till date, 390 chapters have been published of the manga but due to Togashi's health issues, the manga often goes on hiatus and it is still far from being completed.
