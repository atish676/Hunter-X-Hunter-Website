Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Why watch Hunter X Hunter ?
A: Because it's interesting, logical and always with something new. Mr. Togashi do know how to make an unknown turn of events when no one expects it.

Q: Is Hunter X Hunter complete ?
A: No. Neither the manga nor the anime series is completed yet. But the arc till which the anime is completed, it almost seems as if the story is complete. So you may go and watch the anime.

Q: How many episodes Hunter X Hunter got ?
A: 148 episodes (till Chimera Ant arc) in the 2011 version and 62 episodes (till Greed Island arc) in 1999 version.

Q: Is Hunter X Hunter available on Netflix ?
A: Yes. The first 3 seasons of the 2011 version is available on Netflix. Rest of the anime is yet to be available, so I suggest you to watch the full anime on our streaming page in 720p or 1080p :)
